Framework

The Four Pillars of Enterprise KM

A systematic approach to turning scattered information into organizational intelligence.

🔍
Capture
Surface tacit knowledge before it evaporates. Meeting notes, decisions, post-mortems — auto-ingested, never manually filed.
🗂️
Organize
Semantic indexing, not folder hierarchies. AI tags, links, and surfaces relationships no human would manually maintain.
⚡
Retrieve
Natural language search that actually works. Ask a question, get the answer — not a link to a 60-page PDF from 2019.
🔄
Evolve
Knowledge that improves with use. Feedback loops, versioning, and deprecation keep the corpus alive — not a graveyard.
